Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 75 Hard Challenge?
The 75 Hard Challenge is a 75-day program that requires you to complete daily tasks including workouts, water intake, diet management, and photo logging. 75 Hard tracks your progress and helps you stay accountable throughout the challenge.
How do I connect Apple Health?
On your first launch or in Settings, the app will request permission to access Apple Health. Grant the app permission to read your health data including steps, workouts, water intake, weight, and sleep. Your data will automatically sync.
How do I log a workout?
From the home screen, tap the Activity tab and select "Log Workout". Enter your workout type, duration, and intensity. If you have Apple Health or Google Fit connected, your workouts can sync automatically.
Can I track multiple activities daily?
Absolutely! You can log multiple activities throughout the day including workouts, water intake, meals, weight, sleep, mood, and photos. Each activity is tracked separately and contributes to your daily progress.
How do I view my progress?
Navigate to the Analytics tab to view your detailed progress charts, trends, and completion statistics. You can see your daily logs, weekly summaries, and overall challenge progress.
What happens if I miss a day?
If you miss completing all daily requirements, the app will show your streak progress. You can continue from the next day and keep working toward your 75-day goal. The challenge is about building consistency and motivation.
Is my health data secure?
Yes. All your health data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security practices and never share your health information with third parties or advertisers. Your privacy is our priority.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! 75 Hard supports offline mode. You can log your activities while offline, and all changes will automatically sync when you reconnect to the internet.
How do I earn badges?
Badges are awarded automatically as you complete milestones in the 75-day challenge. Complete your daily activities consistently to unlock achievement badges and celebrate your progress.
